Digital Prototype

Try our prototype!



INSTRUCTIONS

This prototype is built as if it is Tuesday afternoon. It will guide you through an initial log in sequence as if you are a first time user. Feel free to click around, but as this is still a very basic prototype there is missing functionality. Not all of the buttons will work. If you click on something that does not work, the buttons that you can click will be momentarily highlighted.

Context/description:
   It's Tuesday. You can vote for what is available for lunch on Thursday because the dining hall needs advance notice to prepare for the most highly voted dishes.
Working features:
   1. Voting for Fettuccine Alfredo on Thursday for lunch.
   2. Logging in and selecting Olin Dining Hall along with vegetarian for dietary preferences.
   3. Viewing the full menu and submitting general comment feedback.
